Who We Are

Fixit is a locally owned power tools business committed to serving Botswana’s builders, creators, and professionals. We focus on providing high-quality power tools for both sale and rental, making reliable equipment accessible to everyone — from construction companies to DIY homeowners.

Our Mission

To provide Botswana with dependable tools that help people work faster, safer, and more efficiently.

What We Offer

  • High-quality power tool sales
  • Flexible rentals for projects or short-term use
  • Accessories and consumables
