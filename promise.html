In construction, downtime is the enemy. FixitBw exists to eliminate it. Our promise isn't just marketing—it's an operational protocol.

Zero Downtime

If a tool rented from us fails during normal operation, we don't just refund you. We deliver a replacement immediately to your site in Gaborone.

Performance Tested

We don't rent "used" tools; we rent "serviced" tools. Every item is inspected, cleaned, and tested for peak voltage/RPM before it leaves our shop.

Honest Rates

No hidden damage waivers or unexplained fees. Our daily rates are transparent, and our purchase prices are competitive with the South African market.

The Maintenance Protocol

We treat our tools like tactical equipment. Every return triggers a rigorous 4-step process before the tool is marked 'Ready'.

Rent Certified Tools
STEP 01

Visual Inspection

Checking for casing cracks, frayed cords, and loose components.

STEP 02

Mechanical Test

Running the tool at max load to ensure motor health and torque stability.

STEP 03

Deep Clean

Removal of concrete dust, grease, and debris from vents and moving parts.

STEP 04

Calibration

Ensuring laser levels are true and blades are aligned perfectly.
